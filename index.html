Our Story

Since 2018, Mama - The Family Restaurant has been a warm, family-owned eatery in the heart of Bhopal. Our goal is simple: to bring together the rich tapestry of Indian regional flavors under one cozy roof. From the creamy gravies of North India to the tangy delights of the South, every dish reflects our passion for authentic, home-style cooking.
